Project A: Bachelor Thesis
 Development of Machine Learning Models for Classification and Segmentation for scatter images of various thin films 

Scatter image of a perovskite thin film with different features 

  • Goal: Automated Analysis of grazing-incidence wide-angle X-ray scattering (GIWAXS) diffraction patterns of perovskites thin films measurements
  • Methods:
  1. classification model, based on ResNet,    is designed to separate diffraction patterns based on the type of Bragg peaks
  2. segmentation model, based on UNet, to automatically recognize and locate rings or diffraction spots in the images
